About the Film

In a fractured timeline full of regret and repetition, Christopher must face the haunting consequence of the choice he can't outrun.

Haunted by a letter. Judged by the kin he left behind. Caught in a surreal cycle where reality and insanity blur.

Christopher's Choice is a cerebral journey into guilt, perception, and the echo of every decision.
